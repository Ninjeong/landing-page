This is Nayeon
Im Na-yeon (born September 22, 1995), known mononymously as Nayeon, is a South Korean singer and a member of the popular girl group TWICE. She is renowned as the group's lead vocalist, center, and "face," known for her cheerful energy and vocal talent.
